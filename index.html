Introducing Istanbul:

Istanbul is a city located in Turkiye, and it acts as a gateway between Europe and Asia. It was once the capital of both the Eastern Roman Empire and the Otttoman Empire. Even now, despite no longer being the capital, Istanbul still is one of the most important cities in the world due to its historical, cultural, and economic importance.
